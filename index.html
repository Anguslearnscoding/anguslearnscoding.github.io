The steak bear

Read time: 20 minutes

Hey there! I'm reaching out because, deep down, I am a foodie through and through. I have been a dedicated connoisseur of all things delicious since 200, and let's just say, eating has always been my professional sport!. Angus's love affair with food began in his grandmother's kitchen, a place filled with the scents of freshly baked bread, simmering stews, and the warmth of family gatherings. As a child, He would spend hours watching his grandmother, a woman who believed that the best meals were made with love and shared with others. These early experiences planted the seeds of Angus's lifelong passion for food.

"Chomp Chomp" - Angus, since 2000

To him, eating is far more than satisfying hunger; its an experience. Every meal is approached with a deep sense of respect and joy, whether its a lavish feast at a fine-dining establishment or a humble bowl of homemade stew. His belief is that food is meant to be savored with all the senses—the visual allure of a well-crafted dish, the fragrance of fresh herbs and spices, the sound of a tender bite, the feel of textures on the palate, and, most importantly, the rich and complex flavors. “I savor every bite,” he says with a grin. “I like to take my time and really experience the layers of flavor and texture. There is something special about allowing the tastes to develop gradually, truly focusing on the sensations happening with each mouthful.”

"Yum Yum" - Angus, 2021

Angus said:

Under the charcoals glowing, steady light, A savory steak calls out into the night, Its charred edges promise pure delight, With every bite, rich juices take their flight.

The steak sizzles, its juices slowly flow, Each cut reveals its tender, savory core, A bite of richness, pure and warm aglow, Leaving me craving just one taste more.

The steaks charred crust, a smoky kiss of fire, With every bite, a world of flavor blooms, Each tender slice fulfills my heart's desire, As rich aromas fill the dining room.

Though diets may frown upon this indulgent fare, In each tender bite, pure bliss I dare to share.

"I got big fat tummy." - Angus, 2024
